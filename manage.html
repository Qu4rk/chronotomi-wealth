Inventory Management

Add, edit, or remove watches from the collection.

How to update the website:

  1. Make your changes to the inventory list below by adding, editing, or deleting watches.
  2. Click the large "Download Updated watches.js" button at the bottom of the page.
  3. Move the downloaded file into your Chronotomi folder, replacing the old watches.js file.
  4. If you added a new watch, make sure to move its picture into the assets folder.
  5. Refresh your website to see the changes.
